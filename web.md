### What is Nanoplasty?
Nanoplasty is the next generation of hair straightening and reconstruction technology.
Developed by Floractive, Nanoplasty utilizes advanced nano-molecular technology and naturally derived active ingredients to transform hair from within. The treatment simultaneously repairs, nourishes, and smooths the hair fiber, delivering long-lasting results without the fumes, discomfort, or harsh chemicals associated with traditional straightening systems.

### The Floractive Story
Founded in Brazil by visionary entrepreneur Jane Sarassi, Floractive has become a global leader in high-performance professional hair treatment.
Since its inception in 2011, Floractive has been committed to developing innovative, safe, and effective cosmetic solutions that combine cutting-edge technology with carefully selected natural ingredients. This commitment has positioned Floractive among Brazil's leading exporters of professional haircare products, trusted by distributors, salon owners, hairstylists, and clients around the world.
Today, Floractive continues to expand internationally, earning recognition for its quality, innovation, and dedication to sustainable beauty.
